Lentil Soup

This comforting lentil soup is chock full of hearty veggies. You can use dried green, brown, or red lentils to make this soup. I like to add a splash of red wine vinegar to bring all the flavors together but you can add fresh lemon juice if you prefer.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 2 cups dried lentils
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • ½ cup spinach, rinsed and thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Step 1 : Gather the ingredients.
  2. Step 2 : Heat oil in a large soup pot over medium heat. Add onions, carrots, and celery; cook and stir until onion is tender, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Step 3 : Stir in garlic, bay leaf, oregano, and basil; cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Step 4 : Stir in lentils, and add water and tomatoes. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and let simmer until lentils are tender, about 40 minutes.
  5. Step 5 : When ready to serve, stir in spinach and cook until it wilts.
  6. Step 6 : Stir in vinegar and season with salt and pepper; taste and adjust as needed.
  7. Step 7 : Serve hot and enjoy!
